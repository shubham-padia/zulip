# Change the time format

Based on your preference, Zulip can display times either in a 12-hour
format (e.g. 5:00 PM) or a 24-hour format (e.g. 17:00).

### Change the time format

<Tabs>
<TabItem label="instructions-for-all-platforms">

1. Click on the **gear** (<i class="zulip-icon zulip-icon-gear"></i>) icon in the upper
   right corner of the web or desktop app.

1. Select **Personal settings**.

1. On the left, click **Preferences**.



1. Under **General**, select your preferred option from the
**Time format** dropdown.

</TabItem>
</Tabs>
