# Change a user's name


!!! warn ""

    This feature is only available to organization owners and administrators.




By default, users can [change their name](/help/change-your-name), though
organization administrators can
[restrict name changes](/help/restrict-name-and-email-changes).

Organization administrators can always change any user's name.

## Change a user's name

<Tabs>
<TabItem label="via-user-profile">

1. Hover over a user's name in the right sidebar.

1. Click on the **ellipsis** (<i class="zulip-icon zulip-icon-more-vertical"></i>)
   to the right of their name to open their **user card**.




1. Click on the **ellipsis** (<i class="zulip-icon zulip-icon-more-vertical"></i>) in the user card.




1. Click **Manage this user**.




1. Under **Name**, enter a new name.


1. Click **Save changes**.





!!! tip ""

    You can also access the **Manage user** tab by clicking the **pencil and
    paper** (<i class="fa fa-edit"></i>) icon at the top of the [user
    profile](/help/view-someones-profile).

</TabItem>
<TabItem label="via-organization-settings">

1. Click on the **gear** (<i class="zulip-icon zulip-icon-gear"></i>) icon in the upper
   right corner of the web or desktop app.

1. Select **Organization settings**.

1. On the left, click **Users**.



1. Find the user you would like to manage. Click the **pencil**
   (<i class="fa fa-pencil"></i>) to the right of their name.

1. Under **Name**, enter a new name.


1. Click **Save changes**.

</TabItem>
</Tabs>
