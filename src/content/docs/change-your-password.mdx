# Change your password

You can reset your password at any time, as long as you either know your
current password, or have access to the email address associated with your
account.

### If you've forgotten or never had a password


<Tabs>
<TabItem label="instructions-for-all-platforms">

1. If you are logged in, start by [logging out](/help/logging-out).

2. Go to your organization's login page at `https://<organization-url>/login/`.

3. Click the **Forgot your password?** link below the **Log in** button or
   buttons.

4. Enter your email address, and click **Send reset link**.

5. You will receive a confirmation email within a few minutes. Open it and click
   **Reset password**.

</TabItem>
</Tabs>




### If you know your current password

<Tabs>
<TabItem label="instructions-for-all-platforms">

1. Click on the **gear** (<i class="zulip-icon zulip-icon-gear"></i>) icon in the upper
   right corner of the web or desktop app.

1. Select **Personal settings**.

1. On the left, click **Account & privacy**.



2. Under **Account**, click on the password field (it should look like `********`).

3. Enter your old password and your new password, and click **Change**.

</TabItem>
</Tabs>
